# Product Context: CapMan AI

## Problem
- **Capacity Bottleneck**: Deep reflection and high-repetition training limited by human bandwidth and market environment
- **Scalability Gap**: Manual model can't generate the volume of unique, complex scenarios needed
- **Engagement & Tracking**: No centralized system to drive engagement and track granular performance data for MTSS framework

## Solution
An AI-driven training platform that:
- Autonomously generates diverse trading scenarios across market regimes
- Grades reasoning quality (not just answers) with probing follow-up questions
- Wraps learning in gamification loops (XP, leaderboards, challenges)
- Automatically classifies learners into MTSS support tiers
- Uses RAG with proprietary CapMan context documents to enforce firm-specific trading logic

## Target Users
- **Students/Traders**: Learning trading concepts through iterative scenario exposure
- **Educators**: Monitoring student progress, identifying intervention needs via MTSS dashboard

## User Experience
- Students face AI-generated trading scenarios, analyze them, and defend their reasoning
- AI probes with follow-ups ("Why did you choose that strike?") and grades the reasoning
- XP and leaderboards create competitive engagement loops
- Head-to-head challenges allow real-time competitive analysis
- Peer review lets users evaluate each other's reasoning
- Educators see a "God View" dashboard with real-time skill-level grouping
